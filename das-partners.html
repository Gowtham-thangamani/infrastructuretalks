DAS and Partners Engineering Consultancy

Company Overview

DAS and Partners Engineering Consultancy is a premier multi-disciplinary engineering firm headquartered in Abu Dhabi, UAE. With over a decade of experience, the firm has established itself as the top-ranked infrastructure consultancy in the region, delivering complex projects that shape the built environment across the Gulf.

From major highway interchanges to utility master plans and coastal marine developments, DAS and Partners combines technical expertise with local regulatory knowledge to ensure projects are delivered on time, within budget, and to the highest international standards.

Beyond core infrastructure, the firm delivers specialist storm water network design, temporary and permanent traffic diversion planning, pipeline and district cooling design, parking design, and buildings structural evaluation — and secures the authority approvals that keep projects moving, including IDAS (Infrastructure Design Approval System) and ITC (Integrated Transport Centre) approvals, NOC permitting, and all certificates required for execution across the UAE.
