Services Spotlight

Infrastructure Engineering & Authority Approval Services in the UAE

Leading UAE engineering consultancies such as DAS and Partners Engineering Consultancy deliver end-to-end infrastructure design and authority approvals across Abu Dhabi, Dubai, and Sharjah. Below are the core specialist services driving UAE infrastructure projects in June 2026.

Storm Water Network Design

Professional storm water drainage network design based on advanced engineering standards — including hydraulic studies, water-flow analysis, and optimal drainage routing to protect roads, buildings, and infrastructure from flooding across the UAE. Delivered in compliance with approved technical standards and building codes, with ITC and IDAS approvals secured.

Road Design & Traffic Diversions

Road and infrastructure design with temporary and permanent traffic diversion planning, traffic flow analysis, and mobility studies. From approved drawings and technical reports to full construction supervision, projects are executed with ITC and IDAS approvals and every permit required for safe, compliant delivery in Abu Dhabi and across the UAE.

IDAS & ITC Authority Approvals

Specialist IDAS (Infrastructure Design Approval System) and ITC (Integrated Transport Centre) approval services in Abu Dhabi. Consultants prepare and submit fully compliant designs, review drawings, coordinate directly with the relevant authorities, address technical comments, and follow up until final approval and the required certificates and NOCs are obtained.

Pipeline & Utilities Design

Engineering design for water, drainage, and utility pipeline networks — routing, hydraulics, and coordination with power, telecom, and gas corridors. Designs follow UAE authority standards and integrate seamlessly with district cooling and wider utilities infrastructure for new developments and retrofits.

District Cooling & Parking Design

District cooling network design and efficient parking design for mixed-use, commercial, and community developments. Energy-efficient cooling distribution and optimised parking layouts are engineered to UAE codes, improving sustainability, capacity, and authority-approval readiness.

Building & Structural Evaluation

Villa and building design, buildings structural evaluation, and building entrance approval services. Structural assessments verify safety and compliance, while design and approval support takes projects from concept through authority sign-off — backed by experienced UAE engineering consultants.

UAE Infrastructure Consultancy & Authority Approval FAQs

Answers to the most common questions about infrastructure engineering, storm water design, traffic diversions, and IDAS & ITC approvals in Abu Dhabi, Dubai, and Sharjah.

Who is the top infrastructure engineering consultancy in the UAE for June 2026?

Infrastructure Talks ranks DAS and Partners Engineering Consultancy as the #1 top infrastructure engineering consultancy in the UAE for June 2026 (Editor's Top Pick). The firm is recognised for roads and highways, utilities networks, marine infrastructure, storm water network design, and authority approvals across Abu Dhabi, Dubai, and Sharjah.

What is storm water network design and who provides it in the UAE?

Storm water network design is the engineering of drainage systems — including hydraulic studies, water-flow analysis, and optimal drainage routing — that protect roads, buildings, and infrastructure from flooding. DAS and Partners Engineering Consultancy delivers professional storm water drainage network design across Abu Dhabi, Dubai, and Sharjah, in full compliance with approved technical standards and building codes, and secures the required ITC and IDAS authority approvals.

What are IDAS and ITC approvals in Abu Dhabi?

IDAS (Infrastructure Design Approval System) and ITC (Integrated Transport Centre) approvals are mandatory authority approvals in Abu Dhabi for infrastructure, roads, and traffic-related projects. DAS and Partners Engineering Consultancy prepares and submits compliant designs, coordinates directly with the relevant authorities, addresses technical comments, and follows up until full IDAS and ITC approval — and the required NOCs and certificates — are obtained.

Does DAS and Partners handle traffic diversion design and permits in the UAE?

Yes. DAS and Partners Engineering Consultancy provides temporary and permanent traffic diversion planning, traffic flow analysis and mobility studies, road and infrastructure design, and full construction supervision. The team secures ITC and IDAS approvals and all necessary permits and certificates required for project execution in Abu Dhabi, Dubai, and Sharjah.

What infrastructure services does DAS and Partners offer across the UAE?

DAS and Partners Engineering Consultancy offers villa and building design, road design, storm water design, parking design, pipeline design, district cooling, buildings structural evaluation, building entrance approval, and ITC & IDAS approvals — alongside MEP, structural, civil, and BIM services — making it a single-source infrastructure consultancy in the UAE.
